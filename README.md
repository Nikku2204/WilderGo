# Wilder GO

**Snap the wild, fill your dex.**

Every tree, bird, squirrel, slug and mushroom around you is a creature waiting to be caught. Wilder GO turns the real world into the game: photograph something wild, flick the ball, and hold your breath while the Ranger decides what you have found.

![Wilder GO onboarding](images/onboarding.webp)

## How it plays

Spot something wild. A street tree counts, so does a weed. Flick the ball when the focus circle is small, and if your aim is true the catch wobbles while the Ranger identifies the exact species, right down to the Latin name. Every catch becomes a collectible card with your photo as the art, a nickname invented for that one sighting, a field entry written on the spot and two abilities true to the species.

Rarity sets the price. A crow pays a little, an orca pays a fortune. New species pay most, repeats pay pocket change, and the third sighting of anything evolves it into a grander form. A fresh quest arrives every morning, achievements pay real Nature Love, and a chest waits at the end of every seven-day streak. You grow from Seedling to Old Growth.

## Why it is different

Most nature apps run a classifier: photo in, label out. Wilder GO runs a naturalist. The same model that identifies your find also names it, writes its card, invents its abilities, judges your daily quest, scouts your neighbourhood and pens its field-guide entry, so no two sightings are ever quite alike. The dex is infinite because reality writes it.

## Features

- A one-flick catch with real throw physics, timing bonuses and a proper wobble
- An infinite dex: any identifiable species, sorted into categories with filter chips, house pets included as Companions
- Nearby Discoveries: three real species scouted daily for your area and season, plus one unnamed mystery
- Home ground and trip mode: type a city at signup, or tell the Ranger you are on a hike, and rumours plus rarity pricing follow you there
- Evolutions at three sightings, with species stacks tracking your full history
- Daily quests written fresh each morning and verified straight from your photo
- Achievements with Nature Love rewards, live progress bars and four medallion families
- Streak chests every seven days, and a week-map trail your ranger walks checkpoint by checkpoint
- A field guide for every species, with a verify-on-Wikipedia link and a strict no-foraging-advice rule
- A fully synthesised soundscape: birdsong, soft piano and flute that give way to battle music mid-catch
- One file, no install, playable from a link

## Field rules

Look, do not touch. The Ranger never gives edibility, medicinal or handling advice, refuses to identify people, and no catch is worth a dash across traffic. Companions count at common rate; the wild pays better. Your home ground is a line of text stored only on your device and used only inside the Ranger's prompts.

## Under the hood

A single React file. Claude Sonnet handles the vision, the judging and every word of prose. Tone.js synthesises the entire soundtrack live, no audio assets exist. Your dex persists between sessions. Mascot, chest, medallions and backdrop art generated with Gemini and ChatGPT, everything else built with Claude.

Part of a weekend series: one small app every weekend, each using AI in a structurally different way.
